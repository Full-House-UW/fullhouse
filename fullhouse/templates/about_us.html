About Us

Our Vision

"Providing roommates an efficient way to manage their daily household lives"

We aim to create an application that will help people living together better manage their lives and be more organized. We want to enrich the communication between roommates, and make assigning everyday household tasks easy for everyone. With Fullhouse, we believe we have accomplished this.

Who We Are

We are a group of college minds come together to form something great. All of us attend the University of Washington and are pursuing a degree in Computer Science. Our fantastic group of seven includes Brandon, Jeff, Cornel, Nat, Shahaf, Conor and our fabulous project lead Janette. Things we enjoy are whiteboards, eating pretzels, head-shaking and tut-tutting (at the same time!), and magic. We have all had the experience of living with other people, and know that it can get hectic! Which is why we bring you this program.

The Process

Much like a large scale software engineering project, this application was developed under realistic specifications and methods. We all thought about what the goal of this application would be, and what we would have to do to get there. We had to consider many different features and structures before we arrived at what you see. There were many milestones that we had to go through and long hours of revisions before we got everything looking how we imagined it. With careful planning and developing, we were able to meet our goals and deliver a finished project.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Full House?

Full House is your digital kitchen whiteboard. It is here to help households manage common tasks-- taking out the trash, cleaning up the kitchen, and more. Full House is here to make commnication between housemates easy and convenient.

How does it work?

Step one, register for Full House and validate your email address.
Step two, create a house if you haven't already.
Step three, invite housemates to your house.
Step four, begin communicating with your housemates!

How much does it cost?

Nothing! It's absolutely free!

What kind of things can I do with Full House?

You can make a house with your friends. You can post announcements to let all your housemates know what is going on. You can create tasks that will cycle through the participating housemates so you always know whose turn it is to empty the dishwasher and who did it last.
